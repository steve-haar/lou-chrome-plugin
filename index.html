Free Chrome Extension

Lou Barcode
Label Generator

Print Avery barcode labels directly from your Lou inventory pages — no copy/paste, no extra software.

Download Extension
Free  ·  No account needed  ·  Chrome only

Install in 4 steps

1

Download & unzip

Click Download Extension above. Once downloaded, find the .zip file in your Downloads folder and double-click it to unzip.

2

Open Chrome Extensions

In Chrome, navigate to:

chrome://extensions

Then enable Developer mode using the toggle in the top-right corner of that page.

3

Load the extension

Click Load unpacked (top-left), then select the unzipped folder you downloaded in step 1.

4

You're done!

Navigate to any SKU page in Lou. You'll see a new Print Label card alongside Edit SKU, Images, and the other buttons. Click it to generate and print your barcode label.

What it does

▁█▂█▁

Code 128 Barcode

Reads the UPC directly from the page and generates a scannable barcode automatically.

💰

Price & Description

Pulls the retail price and product description so your label has everything you need.

🖨️

Avery Label Sheets

Prints a full sheet sized for Avery 5160, 5161, 5163, 5164, 5168, or 5165 templates.

💾

Remembers Your Settings

Your last-used Avery template is saved so you don't have to re-select it every time.
